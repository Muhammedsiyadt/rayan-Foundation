About Rayan Foundation

Rayan Foundation is committed to inclusive education and the development of people with disabilities. Our vision is to offer inclusive education, early intervention, livelihood support, and rehabilitation services for individuals with disabilities such as autism, cerebral palsy, intellectual disability, multiple disabilities, hearing and vision impairment, as well as similar physical and mental conditions.

We also provide palliative and mental health care services, vocational training, assisted living, rehabilitation programs, and research and innovation initiatives to promote accessibility and overall welfare.
